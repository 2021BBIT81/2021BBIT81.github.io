Book Details


Explore the classic adventure of Maurice Sendak's "Where the Wild Things Are," which is currently available at KidBookBazaar. This well-loved picture book, which won the coveted Caldecott Medal, has enthralled kids and adults alike for decades, solidifying its status as an essential addition to every child's library. In this well-known story, young Max dons his wolf suit and sets out for an island home to the mysterious Wild Things, setting off on a fantastical voyage of his imagination. Max is crowned king of the creatures and enjoys a merrymaking until the lure of home draws him back. Sendak's pictures pop off the page with their amazing artwork that perfectly captures every fanciful element, enticing readers to accompany Max on his incredible journey. More than merely a tale, "Where the Wild Things Are" is a celebration of childhood's limitless inventiveness and resiliency. A movie and an opera, among many other adaptations, have been sparked by Sendak's brilliant vision and skillful narrative, guaranteeing that the charm of Max's adventure will never tire from viewers of all ages. Endorsed by Sendak himself, this gorgeous new edition uses cutting-edge technology to faithfully recreate his original artwork, retaining the color and detail that have made his paintings famous. 'Where the Wild Things Are' is still a beloved classic that appeals to readers all across the world, thanks to its fanciful flights of fantasy and its reassuring message of parental love. 'Where the Wild Things Are' from KidBookBazaar will make you feel like you're part of the wild rumpus, and you can join the legions of readers who have embraced this classic narrative.
